My Favourite Movie: Scooby Doo 2: Monsters Unleashed

Scooby Doo 2: Monsters Unleashed follows Scooby and the gang as they resolve
previous mysteries whilst trying to solve the mystery of who recreated all of their
past conquests, how they did it, and most importantly why.
